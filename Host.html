For the first time ever, I’m opening the doors to the Coramino Live Lounge—an exclusive,
members-only speakeasy where you and a guest will get a front row seat to how I live.
This is inner circle stuff. You think I’d open a bottle of Gran Coramino’s finest reposado for
any Joe and Nancy off the street? No! (Unless your name is Joe or Nancy, my apologies.) I
hope you’re as hyped as I am. See you on the other side of that velvet rope.

What you’ll do

The Coramino Live Lounge is my baby. It’s a labor of love and a testament to my hard
work, because it brings together my two greatest passions—comedy and tequila.
(Besides my family , of course. Not trying to get in trouble here.) The vibe is incredible—it’s
old school Hollywood meets Kevin Hart. Like a Rat Pack spot if there were more menu
options than just Beef Wellington. Come hang with me and share a few
laughs (and a few drinks).

Here’s the Kevin Hart-level VIP treatment you can expect.

When you enter through the velvet rope, you’ll be greeted with Gran Coramino’s finest
—cristalinos, añejos, and my personal favorite, reposados—all paired with the perfect
plant-based appetizer. Just a quick heads up—I will say “flavor profile” no less than fifteen
times. I apologize in advance.

I’ll personally guide you through a tasting alongside Chai Lee, the 2023 Gran Coramino
Bartender of the Year. We’ll hook you up with a tasting card that has details on all three
Gran Coramino tequilas, and while you’re reading them, I’m going to steal a reposado
right out from under you. Nah, I won’t do that. (I might, though.) in advance.

Even after the tasting, we’ll have servers walking around all night with our four signature
cocktails—The Boss, The Hart, Summer Melon Spritz, and The Mic Drop. I even got a laser
to etch the Gran Coramino logo onto the ice cubes. That’s how serious I am about this,
people. Lasers!

Our live DJ is gonna bring the beat, so you gotta bring the heat. Just make sure you
stretch first. I want you to tear it up, just not your ACL.

Did you even come if you didn’t snap a pic? Strike a pose for our photographer and let
the ‘gram know I’m taller in person, HA!

To top it all off, we’ve got a killer lineup of all-star performers straight from my contact
list. I’d name drop, but I’m too classy for that. You’ll just have to come and see for yourself.
